About DAVORA Enterprise

Achieving maximum factor productivity and creating maximum stakeholder wealth through ethical investing and entrepreneurial intellectual capacity building is the fundamental mission of the company, which had a humble beginning in 1999.

The company primarily involved in hydro power generation during the inception, steadily grew to a strong and vibrant diversified conglomerate in Sri Lanka, having its footprints in many parts of the world. The group now comprises of 18 companies and employs around 850 direct and over 2000 indirect staff, with few strategic investments, with overall Turn Over of LKR 2.247 billion per annum.
